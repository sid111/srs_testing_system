About SRS Electrical

Pioneering Excellence in Electrical Testing and Lab Automation Since 2005

Leading the Future of Electrical Testing

With over 18 years of excellence, SRS Electrical Appliances has established itself as a trusted name in electrical testing, lab automation, and CPRI-approved certification services. We combine cutting-edge technology with expert knowledge to deliver reliable solutions.

Our Story

Founded in 2005 by electrical engineering pioneers, SRS Electrical Appliances began as a small testing facility in Bengaluru. Today, we have evolved into a state-of-the-art laboratory with national recognition for our testing capabilities and automation solutions.

Our journey started with a simple mission: to provide accurate, reliable, and timely electrical testing services to the growing industrial sector. Over the years, we have invested in advanced testing equipment, trained certified technicians, and developed proprietary automation systems that have set new industry standards.

With a team of over 50 engineers, technicians, and quality assurance professionals, we have successfully tested more than 50,000 electrical products across various categories including switchgear, control panels, capacitors, and specialized electrical equipment.

Our commitment to quality has earned us recognition from CPRI (Central Power Research Institute) and ISO 9001:2015 certification, making us a preferred testing partner for leading electrical manufacturers across India.
