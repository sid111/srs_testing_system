Littelfuse LF15A – 15 A automotive blade fuse Overview

Protect vehicle electrical circuits from overcurrent

Designed for 12 V and 24 V DC systems

Single-use protective device (not resettable)

Available in blade, maxi, glass tube, and ceramic types

Current ratings typically 1 A to 80 A+

Voltage rating usually 32 V DC

Available as fast-acting or time-delay (slow-blow)

Color-coded (blade fuses) to indicate amp rating

Marked with ampere value on body

Blade fuses have two flat terminals, tube fuses have metal end caps

Resistant to vibration, shock, and temperature variations

Mounted in fuse boxes or holders for easy replacement

Prevent wiring damage, component failure, and fire hazards
